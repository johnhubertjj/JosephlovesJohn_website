Intro

Hi! I am a Bristol-based multi-instrumentalist and composer whose work blends melancholy classical textures with the intimacy of contemporary folk.

I also use projections and animations to immerse the audience in the music and deepen the emotional arc of each performance.

If you want to be kept up to date on what I'm up to, sign up below.
