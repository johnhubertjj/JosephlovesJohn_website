Intro:

Hi! I am a Bristol-based multi-instrumentalist and composer whose work blends melancholy classical textures
with the intimacy of contemporary folk. I use projections and animations to fully immerse the audience in the music :).
My music shaped by emotional depth, precise craftsmanship,
and a persistent curiosity about the human condition.
